Lemon Pie

Delicious lemon pie

Lemon meringue pie, lemon meringue pie or lemon pie is a lemon pie, usually served as a dessert, made with a shortcrust pastry crust, filled with lemon cream and a fluffy meringue on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cup Nestlé® Maria Cookies made into crumbs
  • 1/4 Cup Unsalted Butter Melt the butter
  • 1 Can of 397 gr of Condensed Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon peel, grated
  • 3 Units Egg Yolk
  • 3 Units Egg White
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar

Steps

  1. Combine the NESTLÉ® Maria Cookie crumbs with melted butter in a bowl. Place on the base of a mold or individual molds, applying a little pressure.
  2. In a bowl, beat LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk with the lemon juice, lemon zest and egg yolks with a hand mixer until all the ingredients are incorporated. Place the previously prepared NESTLÉ® Maria Cookie base in the mold.
  3. Place in a preheated oven at 180º C, place the preparation and bake for 15 minutes. Once ready, let it cool and refrigerate for a couple of hours before serving. While it cools, beat the egg whites with sugar in an electric mixer until you have a firm meringue. Place in a sleeve and decorate over the filling.
