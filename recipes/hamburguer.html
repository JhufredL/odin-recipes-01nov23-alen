Hamburguer

The best burger

A hamburger is a sandwich made from minced or plant-based meat, bound together in the form of a steak cooked on the grill or grilled, although it can also be fried or baked. Outside the Spanish-speaking world, it is more common to find the American name burger, shortening of hamburger.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Ground Beef
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 Cup BBQ Sauce
  • 4 slices crispy bacon but not burnt
  • 4 Slices Cheddar Cheese
  • 4 Sliced Tomatoes
  • 4 Leaves Lettuce
  • 4 Units Hamburger Bread
  • 1 Tablespoon MAGGI® Mayonnaise to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon MAGGI® Mustard to taste
  • 1 Tablespoon MAGGI® Tomato Sauce to taste

Steps

  1. Place the ground beef in a large container, add garlic, onion and paprika, add half of the Maggi® BBQ Sauce, season with salt, pepper and mix well.
  2. Form the burgers and refrigerate them for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the bacon, separate the cheese and prepare the vegetables.
  4. Cook the burgers on a very hot grill, cover them with the remaining Maggi® BBQ Sauce and finish cooking.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing your favorite sauces on the bread, then the meat, cover it with the cheese and vegetables. Cover the bread and enjoy.
